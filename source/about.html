About

This is a extension made for people who can't control themselves in the internet mainly because of ADHD or the insane amount of distraction online.

This was made by someone who knows how ADHD affects the performance and wants to fix that.


It uses a page-block wall for blocked domains, such as games or social medias by default, but you can change to a whitelist in the configurations Here...

But this extension, instead of fully blocking the page, it asks a reason to access for 5 minutes, for like a mini pause or just seeing something really quickly.

And by asking for a reason, it might make you not get distracted by forcing you to think twice, because the reason and the link you access gets logged and saved


Privacy

Your data is stored localy in the extension's storage

Your data is not sent or saved online

The data collected only includes the blocked domains you access while in able-time¹, the time and the reason

This extension does not read or collect any info from the pages you visit, except for the domain
