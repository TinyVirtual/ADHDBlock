> 
> **Targets**: Google/Chrome, Google/Chromium, Microsoft/EdgeChromium, Opera/Opera, Brave/Brave
> 
> **Risk**: Low

## This extension can:
- Modify and interfere with the pages you visit
- Write data on your device

## Pages Permission:
- Directly block page vizualization
- Inject avaliable time timer in blocked pages
- Read page domain

## Data usage:
- Colected data only includes timestamp, website domain and reason to visit
- Your data is not sold to third party services
- Data from any page visited is not read or stored
- Data is stored localy and not remotely sent anywhere or synced
